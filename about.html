Welcome to my website! I'm a Musician and a Sound Artist who uses computers to shape sound. I'm currently based in Saint-Etienne, France.

I have a background in Computer Music and Music Composition, and I have experience in both the technical and artistic aspects of sound. I am passionate about exploring the creative possibilities of sound and technology, and I am always looking for new ways to push the boundaries of what is possible with sound.

On this website, you can find my portfolio of works, as well as my blog where I share my thoughts on music, sound art, and technology. Contact me if you are interested in collaborating or if you have any questions about my work.

I'm the president of the Cosmonaut Collective, a group of tech artists who explores modern tools to create immersive experiences in which we share our stories about this new cyberpunk world we're living in.
